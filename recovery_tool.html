🔧 OBubba Data Recovery Tool

Scans your Firestore database, finds users whose backup codes point to empty documents, and recovers their data from old-format documents.

⚠️ READ THIS FIRST: This tool will SCAN your database first without making any changes. It will show you a full report. Only when you click "Apply Fixes" will it write anything. Every write is logged so you can see exactly what changed.
